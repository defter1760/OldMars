To secure payment to Attorneys of all sums due under this Agreement, Client hereby grants Attorneys a lien on Client’s claim and any lawsuit filed thereon, and to any recovery Client may obtain, whether by settlement, judgment or otherwise.  Whenever a verdict, judgment, award or settlement is obtained in this matter, the Client hereby gives Attorneys power of attorney to sign the Client’s name to any and all settlements, releases, dismissals, forms, checks, drafts, and other papers, and to deposit any proceeds into the Attorneys’ trust account, and to distribute the funds in accordance with this Agreement.
